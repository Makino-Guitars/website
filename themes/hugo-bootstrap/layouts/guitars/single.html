Standard features:

  • 24.875" scale length
  • 1 11/16” nut width
  • 12” radius
  • 22 Frets
  • Two-piece set neck construction
  • Two-way truss rod
  • Bone nut
  • Switchcraft jack and switch, CTS pots

All guitars are custom built to order, but some options I enjoy include:

  • Available in solid or chambered construction
  • Bridge type: TOM/Stopbar standard, but we can do a stopbar or others.
  • Pickup type and placement: humbuckers or P90s are standard, and we can discuss or modify pickup placement (especially bridge pickup distance from bridge). See my pickup page for details about my pickups - or provide your own.
  • Control format and placement: a 3-way toggle and independent Volume and Tone knobs are standard.
  • Wood: I typically use Spanish Cedar, but can build with a wide variety of woods.
  • Details: inlays, frets, and other details.
  • Finish: typically Nitro, but oil options are available.
